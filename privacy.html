Privacy Policy for AceGuardian

Effective Date: December 30, 2025

AceGuardian ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data within the AceGuardian mobile application.

1. Privacy-First Architecture (On-Device Processing)

AceGuardian is built with a Privacy-First philosophy. To ensure maximum security for our users, all AI analysis, audio processing, and safety monitoring occur locally on your device. No audio, video, or personal safety data is ever transmitted to, or stored on, our servers.

2. Data Collection and Usage

  • Microphone Access: Used solely for real-time safety monitoring via AI. Audio is processed in volatile memory and is never saved or uploaded.
  • Location Data: Used only to provide emergency context. This data stays on your device unless you explicitly trigger a safety alert.
  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers unless you voluntarily provide them for support purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Because we do not store your data on our servers, there is no data "to share."

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is processed on-device and in real-time, we do not retain your personal safety data. Users have full control over the app; deleting the app removes all locally stored configuration data.
